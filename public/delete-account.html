🔒 How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

We fully respect your privacy. To delete your account and all data, follow these steps inside Noor Al-Iman - Quran & Adhan:

⚠️ Important — Data That Will Be Deleted

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. The following will be deleted:

  • Bookmarks (saved verses and pages)
  • Daily Wird data (progress, goals, and reminders)
  • Your settings and preferences (font, theme, language)
  • Your account data (name and email from Google)

📋 Steps to Delete Your Account

1

Open Noor Al-Iman App

Make sure you are signed in with the account you want to delete

2

Go to Settings ⚙️

Tap the Settings icon on the home screen

3

Tap "Account"

Find the Account section at the bottom of the Settings list

4

Select "Delete Account and Data"

This option is at the bottom of the Account page

5

Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message, then tap "Delete Permanently" to confirm

6

Done

Your account and all data are deleted immediately after confirmation

📊 Data Deleted vs. Retained

We are fully transparent about what we store:

⏱️ Retention period: When deleted from within the app, all data is erased immediately with no backups retained. When deleted via the form below, processing takes up to 7 business days.
