🔒 How to Delete Your Account

We respect your privacy. If you want to delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

⚠️ Important Notice

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your app data, including:

  • Bookmarks and notes
  • Your preferences (language, theme, etc.)
  • Listening and reading history
  • Login information

will be permanently deleted.

📋 Steps to Delete Your Account

1

Open the App

Launch the "Noor Al-Iman" app on your device

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) on the home screen

3

Find "About" or "My Account"

Look for "Account" or "About" option at the bottom of the Settings menu

4

Tap "Sign Out"

Multiple options will appear - select "Delete Account and Data"

5

Confirm Deletion

You'll be asked to confirm. Tap "Delete Permanently" to proceed

6

Done

Your account and all your data will be deleted within 90 days

  • Backup records (deleted after 90 days)
